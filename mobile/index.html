Samsung Galaxy A54 is the newest product in the Samsung A series after the launch of the Samsung A53 in February 2022. The availability of this cool gizmo at a handy price makes it an excellent deal to lock in for the season.
        
Samsung Galaxy A54 price in India is expected to be from ₹ 22,299. This mobile is rumoured to be an excellent phone with Exynos 9611 (10nm) processor, 6 GB / 8 GB of RAM, and 128 GB of storage.
        
Design and Display
This mobile will run on Android 11 and will have a decent resolution of 1080 x 2400 pixels, 20:9 ratio.
        
Performance
Samsung Galaxy A54 packs Exynos 9611 (10nm) processor and it will have Octa-core (4x2.3 GHz Cortex-A73 & 4x1.7 GHz Cortex-A53) CPU an Mali-G72 MP3 GPU.

Camera
In terms of camera, this model is supposed to come up with rear camera of 48 MP and front camera of 32 MP (f/2.2. Other camera features include LED flash, panorama, HDR

In connectivity, this model will include Edge, GPRS, 2G, 4G, 5G. Different network support available for the 3g: HSDPA 850 / 900 / 1900 / 2100 HSDPA 850 / 900 / 1700(AWS) / 1900 / 2100.
